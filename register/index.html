Important Information

Airport transfers

Airport transfers from and to airport on the 27th and the 31st can be arranged if required – booking before December required and separate fees applied.

 

Disabilities

People with special needs will need to ensure that your requirements are known at the time of registration.

Vietnamese cuisine will be the main dishes provided at the Melbourne 2016 conference. Special dietary requirements must be made known at the time of registration – we cannot guarantee all different dietary needs can be met.
