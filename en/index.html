Vietnamese Christians Fellowship Conference in Australia – Melbourne 2016

 

This is the 5th Bi-Annual Australia wide Conference, organized by a number of Vietnamese Evangelical denominations in Australia. Melbourne 2016 Conference is organized by the participating churches in Victoria, with the Vietnamese Kew Baptist Church holding the key responsibility.

The conference will begin on the evening of December 27, 2016 and finishes at at noon December 31, 2016.

Registration Info

Fees

  • Under 5: $50 AUD
  • 6-12 y.o: $350 AUD
  • 12-64 y.o: $440 AUD
  • Above 64/Pension: $390 AUD
Family discount:

Family with 2 parents & 2 or more children gets family discount. First oldest child full fee according to standard fee.

Second child and any other child after:

  • under 5 -> free
  • Over 5 ->$100 off
Air discount

$20 money back plus the air bed itself for those who sleep on the air bed.

Fee includes all costs at Conference: accommodation, food (Vietnamese cuisine – catered by church) and all facilities during the conference period.

Transport: Airport transfers from and to airport on the 27th and the 31st can be arranged if required – booking before December is required and $25/ AUD both ways.

Dietary: Vietnamese cuisine will be the main dishes provided at the Melbourne 2016 conference. Special dietary requirements must be made known at the time of registration – we cannot guarantee all different dietary needs can be met.
